Germán Rosati

I am specialized in Machine Learning methods applied to Social Ssciences, particularly to study social and agrarian structure and to text mining

I am Assistant Researcher at Consejo Nacional de Investigaciones Científicas y Técnicas -CONICET- (awaiting my appointment) based in the Instituto de Altos Estudios Sociales of National University of San Martín (IDAES-UNSAM).

I also teach Quantitative Methods as Associate Professor in IDAES-UNSAM, at pre-graduate studies in Sociology and Anthropology

Previously I have been head of Data Science and Artifitial Intelligence programs at Digital House Coding School. I have also led Data Production Area at PASCAL Program in UNSAM.

I have worked as Expert Data Analyst in the Ministry of Labor, Employment and Social Security (Argentina)

I have taught several graduate and postgraduate courses in Data Science, Machine Learning and Statistical Methods

I have spent several months as Invited Researcher at Freie Universität Berlin and Iberoamerican Institute of Berlin.

I hold a PhD. in Social Sciences, a Bachelor degree in Sociology (University of Buenos Aires) and M.Sc. in Generation an Analysis of Statistical Information (UNTREF).
